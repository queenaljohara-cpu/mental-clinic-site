Ethical User Agreement

Before completing your booking request, you must agree to the following terms to ensure privacy, confidentiality, ethical communication, and responsible system use.

Agreement Policies

1. Privacy & Confidentiality

  • All personal information is treated as confidential.
  • Electronic records are protected using secure systems.
  • No sharing of patient data without consent.

2. Accurate Information

  • Users must provide correct and honest information.
  • Impersonation or fake identities are forbidden.

3. Responsible System Use

  • No attempts to hack, modify or damage the system.
  • No harmful or abusive content may be sent.
  • The system is used for ethical and legal purposes only.

4. Respectful Communication

  • Communication must be respectful and professional.
  • Hate speech or harassment is not allowed.

5. Data Protection

  • Your data will never be sold or misused.
  • Access is restricted to authorized staff only.
  • Users must secure their own devices.

6. Emergency Cases

  • This website is not an emergency service.
  • Contact local emergency numbers for urgent situations.

Final Confirmation

Please confirm that you have read and agree to the Ethical User Agreement.
